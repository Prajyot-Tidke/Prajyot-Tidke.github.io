Academic Resource Hub

Hi, I'm Prajyot. I am currently in the 11th grade at Ashoka Universal School, studying under the ISC board. I've created this centralized hub to track and share the most effective study materials for JEE Mains & Advanced, as well as the SAT and IELTS for my future undergraduate studies abroad.

JEE Mains & Advanced

Core materials supplementing standard IIT coaching curriculum for competitive engineering exams.

  • Physics: Concepts of Physics by H.C. Verma (Vol 1 & 2) for foundational mechanics and electromagnetism.
  • Physics (Advanced): Problems in General Physics by I.E. Irodov for high-level problem solving.
  • Complete PHYSICS in One Shot for JEE Main + Advanced
  • Chemistry: NCERT textbooks for strictly adhering to the inorganic chemistry syllabus.
  • Chemistry (Organic): MS Chouhan for extensive reaction mechanism practice.
  • Complete CHEMISTRY in One Shot for JEE Main + Advanced
  • Mathematics: Cengage Learning series (G. Tewani) for comprehensive calculus and coordinate geometry.
  • Mock Tests: Previous Years' Questions (PYQs) from the last 10 years for exam pattern familiarity.
  • Complete MATHS in One Shot for JEE Main + Advanced

SAT Preparation

Digital SAT strategies focusing on the Reading/Writing and Math modules.

  • Official Practice: The College Board Bluebook app for full-length adaptive practice tests.
  • Targeted Drills: Khan Academy's official Digital SAT course for personalized skill-building.
  • Math Review: College Panda's SAT Math for advanced algebraic concepts and problem-solving shortcuts.
  • English Grammar: Erica Meltzer's SAT Grammar guide for mastering standard English conventions.
  • Vocabulary: Curated Quizlet flashcards targeting high-frequency SAT vocabulary in context.

IELTS Prep

Frameworks and practice tests to secure a high band score across all four language modules.

  • Authentic Tests: Cambridge IELTS Academic Student's Books (11 through 18) for realistic exam conditions.
  • Writing Strategy: IELTS Liz online tutorials for structuring Task 1 (Data) and Task 2 (Essays).
  • Speaking Practice: Mock interview prompts focusing on current cue card topics to build fluency.
  • Listening: BBC 6 Minute English podcasts to improve comprehension of various accents at natural speeds.
  • Reading: Strategies for skimming, scanning, and identifying True/False/Not Given traps.
